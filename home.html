Introduction --- **AMI** (**A**TLAS **M**etadata **I**nterface) is a generic ecosystem for metadata aggregation, transformation and cataloging. In a Small or Big Data contexts, it provides high-level capabilities to retrieve scientific data by metadata criteria. AMI benefits from more than 20 years of feedback in the LHC context at CERN. Last news are on the [AMI ecosystem Web site](https://ami-ecosystem.in2p3.fr).
